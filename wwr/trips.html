Our Featured Adventures

Colorado River Classic

Duration: 4 hours | Difficulty: Intermediate

Experience the legendary Colorado River with its world-famous rapids. This classic adventure takes you through stunning red rock canyons and challenging Class III-IV rapids. Perfect for those seeking an authentic white water experience with breathtaking scenery.

  • Professional certified guides
  • All safety equipment included
  • Lunch and refreshments provided
  • Photo package available
$89 per person

Green Wall Rapids Challenge

Duration: 6 hours | Difficulty: Advanced

For the thrill-seekers! Navigate through the most challenging rapids in the region. This advanced trip features Class IV-V rapids and requires previous rafting experience. Conquer the legendary Green Wall and other heart-pounding rapids.

  • Advanced safety briefing
  • High-performance equipment
  • Professional photographer included
  • Certificate of completion
$149 per person

Family Fun Adventure

Duration: 3 hours | Difficulty: Beginner

Perfect for families with children 8 and up! This gentle introduction to white water rafting features calm stretches and mild Class I-II rapids. Enjoy scenic views, wildlife spotting, and create lasting family memories.

  • Family-friendly guides
  • Child-sized safety gear
  • Snacks and drinks included
  • Wildlife identification guide
$65 per person
